Understanding Inflammation and Systemic Infections

Exploring the genes and pathways that drive inflammatory responses, unlocking insights for better healthcare.

What are Systemic Infections?

Systemic infections affect the entire body by spreading through the bloodstream, often triggering widespread inflammatory responses. These infections can lead to severe conditions such as sepsis, organ failure, or even death.

What are Inflammatory Responses?

Inflammatory responses are critical immune reactions to injury or infection. They protect the body by eliminating pathogens and initiating tissue repair but can also lead to harm if dysregulated.
